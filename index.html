Italo American Lodge

The Italo-American Lodge is a fraternal organization that celebrates Italian culture and heritage. The lodge was founded in 1949 and since then has become a pillar for the community providing and performing social, educational, charitable, recreational, fraternal, and other similar services for its members and for the benefit of the community in general.
